Bonus section: hardware versus software

  • Article
  • 03/28/2024

This bonus section will describe the difference between hardware and software.

Hardware is the physical technology that makes computers inside of devices work. In other words, hardware is the computer. However, software works on top of that and is the functionality developed to run on the computer. This means that anything that uses the computer to do something is a form of software. Where this makes a difference is the differentiation of fields where computer science generally refers to work relating to software and computer engineering refers to work relating to hardware.
